Welcome

This a website with games and activities about the Mi'gmaq language. Mi'gmaq is an Algonquian language spoken across Eastern Canada, with communities in New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, Quebec, Prince Edward Island, and New Foundland and Labrador. These games are meant to give beginners a taste of the language and be fun for all.
